Welcome to Hogwarts Adventures

It was a day like any other. Except for the strange letter that arrived on your doorstep this morning when an owl nearly ran into your front door. Your curiosity gets the best of you and you find yourself cracking the red wax seal and staring at what seems to be a handwritten note detailing your acceptance to Hogwarts School of Witchcraft & Wizardry this coming school year.

Designed for players new to tabletop RPGs, Hogwarts Adventures relies on skills, decision-making, and spellcasting more than on traditional battle solutions. After all, you are 11 and you can’t even own your own broom during your first school year. Grab your shiny dice, remember how to do basic math, and remember that the main objective for both you and your players is to have fun while drinking mimosas and creating wonderful memories.

So pick your wand and pack your trunk because you are on your way to more than just school:
you are on your way to Hogwarts Adventures!
